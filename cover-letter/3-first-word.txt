Rules of the first word creating:
- do not specify the field of the business (robotics, medicine etc.). Specify the field of the development (memory optimization, AI, design patterns etc.). 
- create first using 1-2 first points of "Обязанности" block
- describe something interesting in the field of the vacancy according to development (difficult tasks, high apportunities for grows etc.)
- start from the text "Здравствуйте! Меня заинтересовала эта вакансия, так как"
